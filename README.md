# Reliance Digital: Retail Sales & Customer Insight Analysis

Welcome to the official repository for the Reliance Digital Data Analysis Project. This project is designed to simulate a real-world business scenario where you act as a Data Analyst for one of India's largest electronics retailers.

In this repository, you will find all the resources required to build a comprehensive Power BI/Tableau dashboard from scratch.

# üöÄ Project Overview
Problem Statement: You are working at Reliance Digital and have been tasked to build a dashboard for the executive leadership team. The goal is to analyze sales performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments to drive data-driven decision-making.

# Key Objectives:
Track 6 core KPIs: Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Quantity, Average Transaction Value (ATV), Transaction Count, and Loyalty Penetration Rate.

Analyze revenue trends across Years, Quarters, and Months.

Identify top-performing brands and categories.

Understand regional sales distribution across India.

# üìÇ Repository Structure
This repository is organized as follows:

Datasets/: Contains the raw CSV files used for the analysis.

customers.csv: Customer demographics and loyalty info.

products.csv: Product details, categories, and costs.

stores.csv: Store locations and types.

sales_2023.csv, sales_2024.csv, sales_2025.csv: Historical transaction records.

Project_Problem_Statement.md: The detailed agenda and requirements for the project.

Scripts_and_Codes/: Contains VBA scripts used for Slicer.

Final_Dashboard/: The final dashboard file.

# üìä Business Questions to Solve
To complete this project, your dashboard must answer:

Revenue Trends: How is the revenue trending across years, quarters, and months? (Line Chart)

Category Performance: Which categories contribute most to the revenue?

Geographical Distribution: How are sales distributed across different regions/states?

Customer Segmentation: What is the revenue contribution from Budget, Mid-range, and Premium segments?

Brand Popularity: Which brands are the top performers in terms of quantity sold?

# üí° Summary of Insights
Upon completion, your dashboard summary should highlight:

The highest growth period for Reliance Digital.

The most dominant region in terms of sales volume.

The impact of the Loyalty Program on total revenue.
